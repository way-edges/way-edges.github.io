## Available widgets

`<type> - <widget>`

- `btn` - Button
- `slide` - Slide
  - `speaker` - Speaker
  - `microphone` - Microphone
  - `backlight` - Backlight
- `box` - Box
  - `ring` - Ring (_Provide presets_)
  - `text` - Text (_Provide presets_)
